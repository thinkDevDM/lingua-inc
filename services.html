Language Lessons

The language lessons we provide have been rated in the top 5 worldwide. After many months of rigorous research, trial and error, we've developed our own unique method to language learning. And it works!

Our approach is simple, yet comprehensive. We take you through a 3-phase curriculum, with each phase utilizing all four ingredients of learning a new language: 1) Listening, 2) Speaking, 3) Reading, 4) Writing. However, each phase has its own emphasis and increases in difficulty. This approach ensures that you receive a well rounded experience in your target language and conclude your training head and shoulders above the students of many other language organizations or programs. What's more, you won't be drilled with endless grammar rules (boring!). Rather, you'll be briefly introduced to key grammar rules here and there as you progress through the lessons, allowing you to better comprehend and manipulate your target language.

Here's what you'll get through our language training:

  • A comprehensive study guide full of high-frequency vocabulary, conversational reading exercises, translation exercises, cultural information, and grammar notes, in a PDF format.
  • Multiple audio files of increasing diffulty to practice your listening comprehension
  • One-on-one or small group (max of 5 people) lessons, thus allowing for optimal time with your instructor. You will have 3-5 sessions each week ranging from 1-2 hours a session.
  • Verbal and written testing at the end of each phase to gauge your progress and discern where your strengths and weaknesses lie.
  • A professional certification exam, recognized by numerous professional organizations, that can be completed (at no additional cost to you) up to 4 months after you complete phase three of your language lessons. There is a $150 charge after the 4 month deadline.

Interpretation

We provide both consecutive and simultaneous interpretation. Consecutive interpretation is where a person speaks in one language and then pauses for the interpreter to convey what was said. Simultaneous interpretation is common in conferences where multiple languages are often needed for proper communication (e.g. United Nations). The interpreter communicates in the target language through the use of headphones, thus allowing the speaker to continue without interruption.

We can accomplish these interpretation approaches in person, over the phone, or over some other virtual means. We do ask that you provide us with at least six hours of notification prior to the start time of the meeting. This allows us to strategize logistically and review the subject matter to be discussed. The more time we have to plan, the better service we can provide.

Translation

We provide translation services for just about anything you can think of. From something as simple as a single page website to something as complex and involved as a legal document, we've got your translation needs covered. Our translators are highly skilled linguists with numerous years in the business. Many of them have worked in various professional fields (e.g. law; business; web development) prior to entering the translation profession.

We utlize specialy designed software to meet your translation needs, allowing for rapid progress and accurate results. What is more, we utilize a two-person system -- a translator and an editor/reviewer -- so as to ensure the best results possible.
